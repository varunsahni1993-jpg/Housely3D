# Research Documentation Structure

## Purpose

Define how research is organized so experiments, references, and findings can be reused.

## Structure

- Research question.
- Source collection.
- Experiment design.
- Findings and recommendations.
- Follow-up actions and open questions.

## Dependencies

- AI documentation plan.
- Documentation standards.
- Decision logs.

## Review Checklist

- Is the research question explicit.
- Are sources traceable.
- Are conclusions separated from observations.

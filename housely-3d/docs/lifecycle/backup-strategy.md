# Backup Strategy

## Purpose

Document how documentation, decisions, and supporting assets are preserved and recoverable.

## Rules

- Keep backups policy-driven, not ad hoc.
- Separate backup retention from production working copies.
- Make recovery steps explicit and testable.

## Dependencies

- Versioning strategy.
- Release strategy.
- Decision logs.

## Review Checklist

- Is backup scope clear.
- Are recovery expectations documented.
- Is retention policy stated.

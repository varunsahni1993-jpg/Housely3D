# Chapter 03 - Domain Constraints

## Purpose

Capture the invariants and practical limits that shape product behavior.

## Deliverables

- Constraint list.
- Non-negotiable rules.
- Assumption register.

## Dependencies

- Workflow map.
- Architecture plan.

## Estimated Size

- Small to medium.

## Review Checklist

- Are constraints grounded in business or technical reality.
- Are assumptions tagged.
- Could the constraints change later.

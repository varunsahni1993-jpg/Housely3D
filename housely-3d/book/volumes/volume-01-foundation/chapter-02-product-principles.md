# Chapter 02 - Product Principles

## Purpose

Define the principles that govern product decisions across design, AI generation, and cost estimation.

## Deliverables

- Principle list.
- Decision examples.
- Non-goals.

## Dependencies

- Vision and mission chapter.
- Documentation standards.

## Estimated Size

- Small to medium.

## Review Checklist

- Are principles durable.
- Do they cover tradeoffs the team will actually face.
- Are the non-goals explicit.
